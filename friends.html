Celebrating fun and memories with my closest friends.

This picture was taken at a cozy coffee shop in Guiguinto, Bulacan, where my elementary friends and I finally managed to get together after a long time. It’s funny how one of our friends, the one wearing blue, just joined us out of nowhere—it’s become a joke we all love! Even though our schedules rarely line up, nothing changes in our friendship.
