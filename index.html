About Me

Hi! I'm Arabella D. Francisco but you can call me Ara or Aira. I am a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Dr. Yanga's Colleges, Inc. My journey through college sparked a deep passion for technology, problem-solving, and innovation. During my academic years, I honed my skills in programming, system development, and exploring the vast world of computer engineering. Now, as I take the next step into the professional world, I’m eager to apply my knowledge and enthusiasm to real-world projects and grow as a tech professional.
